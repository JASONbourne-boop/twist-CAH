How To Play
1. The Setup: Nominate a Game Master to keep score. Put the tablet in the middle of the table. This game uses Cards Against Humanity cards. Everyone grabs a 15 white cards.
2. The Draw: The first player is the "Linesman." They tap either Official or Custom to draw a spectrum and read it out loud to the table.
3. The Pitch: Every other player picks one white card from their hand that fits somewhere on that spectrum and hands it face-down to the Linesman.
4. The Lineup: The Linesman shuffles the submitted cards and flips them face-up on the table. Assign a letter to each card from left to right (A, B, C, D, E, etc.).
5. The Secret Order: The Linesman secretly decides the "correct" order of those cards along the app's spectrum (Left to Right).
6. The Guess: Everyone else looks at the letters and secretly writes down what they think the Linesman's exact order is (Example: C - A - E - D - B).
7. The Reveal & Scoring: The Linesman reveals their secret order. Players get 1 point for every card they placed in the exact right spot. The Game Master tallies the points, the person to the left becomes the new Linesman, and you draw a new spectrum.
Game Length & Point Goals 20 Points: A quick game. Roughly 30-45 minutes. 50 Points: The standard session. Roughly 1.5 to 2 hours. 100 Points: A marathon. You are going to be here all night. Order a pizza.
